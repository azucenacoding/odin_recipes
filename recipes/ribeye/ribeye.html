Ribeye Steak

Recipe Description

This ribeye recipe is simple, only taking 20 minutes at most with only a few simple ingredients for a perfect dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1lb 1.5-inch thick ribeye steak
  • Kosher salt
  • Black pepper
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Rosemary (for added flavor, purely optional!)

Recipe Steps

  1. Firstly, season your steaks with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a cast-iron skillet over high heat until it's smoking.
  3. Add the steak to the skillet. (If you don't hear an immediate sizzle, get the steak out of the pan until the skillet is fully heated!)
  4. Cook each side for 30 seconds on high heat.
  5. Turn down the heat to medium-high and cook each side for 2 minutes. Add butter and garlic for baste.
  6. Remove the steak from the pan onto a plate. With a thermometer, check the center temperature for 135F or higher. If the temperature is not immediate, place it back in the pan to cook a bit longer.
  7. Let the steak rest for 5-10 minutes before slicing.
  8. Enjoy your delicious rib-eye steak!
