Spaghetti and Meatballs

Recipe Description

This recipe is a timeless classic, only taking 35 minutes to make. Easy to prep, but wonderful to enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1lb spaghetti
  • Salt for pasta water
  • 1 1/4 pounds of ground sirloin
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/2 cup of italian breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 chopped garlic cloves
  • Salt and pepper
  • A jar of marinara sauce

Recipe Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425F. Place a large pot of water to boil for the spaghetti. Once the water starts boiling, add salt and pepper along with the pasta noodles and let it cook to al dente.
  2. Mix beef and Worcestershire, egg, bread crumbs, cheese, garlic, salt and pepper. Roll meat into 1 1/2 inch medium-sized meatballs and place on nonstick cookie sheet or a cookie sheet greased with extra-virgin olive oil. Bake balls 10 to 12 minutes, until no longer pink.
  3. Toss hot, drained pasta with a few spoonfuls of the sauce and grated cheese. Turn meatballs in remaining sauce. Place pasta on dinner plates and top with meatballs and sauce and extra grated cheese.
